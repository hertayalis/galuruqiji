Lacey Evans Wants To Be A Good Stepmom For Charlotte Flair

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, June 3, 2024

Lacey Evans and Ric Flair made their new union official on WWE Raw this week, and The Lady Of WWE wants to be a classy step mother.

Evans tweeted out a photo of a book cover along with a caption. She looking to read up before diving headfirst into this new chapter in her life. The book in question was: “The Stepmoms’ Club: How to Be a Stepmom without Losing Your Money, Your Mind, and Your Marriage.”

Looking into some new reading material. Any suggestions on the subject would be helpful! Thank you all in advance. xoxo #MamaFlair

This new storyline will put Ric Flair on WWE television much more often. He is also opposing his real-life daughter at the same time. The Nature Boy already had Evans doing the Ric Flair drip last night. Let’s see where this angle goes from here,
