Sofia Wylie Biography, Personal Data, Age, Parents and Family Facts

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, August 8, 2024

Surely anyone who watches Disney Channel’s “Andi Mack” TV series would love to know more about the gorgeous teenage actress who plays the role of Buffy Driscoll so effortlessly. This stunning on-screen wonder is only portrayed by Sofia Wylie, who is not only an actress but has also ventured into dance, modelling, producing and music. Below all there is to know about her. biography, age, parents and personal data.

Sofia Wylie Biography (Age)

Sofia was born in Scottsdale, Arizona in the United States of America. His birth took place on January 7, 2004 in the family of Chris and Amy Wylie. The young actress is also a dancer, actress, musician and is now experimenting with her talent in production.

At the age of five, Sofia’s already expressed interest in dancing prompted her parents to enroll her in the Diverse Elements Dance Studio for her dance lessons. She became a good dancer and she appeared in various television commercials. Sofia finally had the opportunity to perform on stage with world famous young music star, Justin Bieber, during his Purpose World Tour. She soon started performing as a dancer in highly entertaining American TV series, such as “You Think You Can Dance” (2011 & 2016), “Dance Moms” and “America’s Got Talent” (2015). In 2017, she launched an online dance series with dancers from Utah, and in 2019, she launched an IGTV educational dance series called “Dancing with Sofia Wylie.”

Along with her career as a dancer, Sofia is also an actress who has acted in many films. She studied acting at the Second City Training Center in Hollywood, California. She attended a few auditions before getting a recurring role on the Nickelodeon comedy series, “Nicky, Ricky, Dicky, and Dawn.” She was also part of the cast of another comedy series “School of Rock”. In 2016, it was announced that Sofia would be taking on a major acting role as Buffy Driscoll, one of Andi’s best friends and a major character on the Disney Channel’s Andi Mack series, making her revealed to the public. fame in the entertainment industry.

Sofia also had the lead role of Cory Bailey in ‘Back of the Net’, a 2019 Australian film. The film was released in Australia and the United States. Wylie also performed the role of Riri Williams in an animated TV movie titled “Marvel Rising: Heart of Iron” which aired on Disney XD. She is set to play the role of Gina in “High School Musical: The Musical,” a Disney+ series, as announced in February 2019.

Sofia has also excelled as a model and has been featured in popular magazines such as Deadline, InStyle, Teen Vogue and Elle Magazine. She is also into music. In January 2019, she released her debut single, “Side by Side,” for “Marvel Rising: Chasing Ghosts.” She is also a producer since she launched AIFOS, a production company in March 2019, which is to carry out her first project, “The Fall of Innocence”, based on the novel by Jenny Torres Sanchez.

Personal Information – Parents and Family Information

Her father, Chris, makes a living as a life coach, and he must have had a big influence on Sofia’s career decisions. Her mother, Amy, may also have had a major influence on Sofia’s choice of cuisines and dishes, which could be the secret to her lustrous looks.

The couple is blessed with two daughters, Sofia being the youngest daughter. Sofia’s older sister is Isabella Wylie. Isabella is also a celebrity, having won the Chopped Junior Show aired on the Food Network. She is a cook who, after many auditions, has become one of the best chefs.

The gorgeous sisters have been compared to the Kardashian brothers and their mutual respect cannot be ignored.
