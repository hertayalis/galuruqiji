Kirsten Dunst Baby Name Ennis Howard Plemons

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, July 20, 2024

Kirsten Dunst and her fiancé, actor Jesse Plemons, welcomed their first child, a baby boy, on May 3, 2018. And now, we finally know the baby's name.

Related Story

According to a new report in The Blast, Dunst and Plemons named their baby boy Ennis. The publication also obtained a copy of the birth certificate, which reveals that the baby's full name is Ennis Howard Plemons.

Dunst revealed her pregnancy in January, by posing in a gorgeous photoshoot for Rodarte:

As for the name's meaning, Ennis means "Island" and also happens to be the name of a town on an island in County Clare, Ireland.

Dunst and Plemons have yet to reveal why their chose the beautiful name, but it could be inspired by how they met, as Ennis was the name of a character in Fargo Season 3. Dunst and Plemons famously met on the set of Season 2 of the show, in which they played a married couple.

Getty Images

Plemons is, of course, also famous for playing Todd in Breaking Bad, and Landry in Friday Night Lights.

Since meeting Plemons, Dunst has spoken about her desire to have children, telling Marie Claire UK (via E!) in May 2017, "I'm at a stage in my life where I'm like, I've been working since I was 3. It's time to have babies and chill. You know what I mean?"

Dunst also revealed:

"I wasn't one of those 'I need a baby!' people until my goddaughter was born. I love her so much. That love is just like… you can’t experience that unless you have a kid."
