LeBron James' Mom Gloria &amp; Wife Savannah Pose Together Makeup Free See How Stunning They Look

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, June 9, 2024

From his mother to his wife to his daughter, LeBron James truly knows how to spread the love to all the women in his life. Recently, his mom took to Instagram, showing off her warm relationship with his spouse.

LeBron James' wife Gloria Marie James took to social media, sharing a post that included a snapshot of her daughter-in-law Savannah Brinson. They both looked stunning posing make-up-free in the sweet Instagram picture.

Their skin looked flawless, glowing golden brown, as their attire matched the tone of their complexion. Brinson even sported a glam beanie accentuating the chill-elegant style exuding from both mother and daughter.

Savannah and LeBron James at the Sports Illustrated Sportsperson of the Year Ceremony on December 12, 2016 in New York. | Photo: Getty Images

A BLESSED EASTER

Beyond the comfy clothes, both Gloria and Brinson appeared intensively relaxed in one another's company, perhaps implying a healthy and intimate relationship between the in-laws. Penning the caption, Gloria wrote a sweet tribute:

"Hoping you all had a blessed and safe #eastersunday2021! From my family to yours ?."

Both of their dark eyes twinkled as they stared deeply into the camera lens with wide and authentic smiles stretched across their faces. Brinson donned a light pink tracksuit while Gloria sported a blue tie-dye overall with black pants.

A FAMILY THAT STICKS TOGETHER

The other two photos included pictures of Gloria with the rest of James' children and Gloria's grandkids. These are "Bronny" James Jr., Bryce Maximus James, and, lastly, little Zhuri James.

Brinson expressed that she is incredibly respectful to him, his mom, and their kids.

James and his mother's fans filled the comment section with adoration, although many asked where her famous son was. The Instagram share received an abundance of likes, with many complimentary emojis.

A MOM'S STRENGTH

James and his mother share an infinitely deep bond, based on the hardships they had to struggle through together. Taking to FaceTime, James contacted Gloria following an NBA championship win to express his gratitude towards her:

"You are the reason why I'm able to even do this, Mom. You don't understand."

Gloria gave birth to the now superstar basketball player when she was just 16 years old and a single mother. In his FaceTime with his mom, he congratulated her on her strength in raising him in such difficult conditions.

THE POWER OF A WOMAN

Perhaps because of his mother, James is attracted to strong women such as his wife. Brinson has stuck by the basketball player's side through many lows and, of course, enjoyed the good times as well.

Speaking about how her husband treats her, Brinson expressed that she is incredibly respectful to him, his mom, and their kids. The two are high school sweethearts and have seven children together.

One of these children is their little girl Zhuri who celebrated her 6th birthday last year. Naturally, the two loving parents didn't hesitate to pull out all the stops when it came to celebrating their precious child's special day.

The day was filled with multi-colored balloons and other decorations, her name written via balloons in an arch-like shape. Brinson also allowed Zhuri to have a cup of "coffee" that day to commemorate her growing up.
