Biography

Tony Glavin was born in Scotland on April 29, 1958. In the 1970s and 1980s, this professional soccer midfielder played for the Scottish Football League teams Hamilton Academical and Queen’s Park and went on to play for the North American Soccer League team the Philadelphia Fury. He became a coach after retirement. He and Rick Davis both played for the St. Louis Steamers. From 1975 to 1976, he played his youth career for Queen’s Park.
