We know that you like playing Sports Games, and water sports are not only fun, but some of them are relaxing or exciting, but the thing that you will be doing in this new game is certainly going to be on the exciting side of things, as we are talking about a game entitled Flip Diving, which is a game in which you dive into water by doing flip, which is honestly the most fun way that you can dive in the water, isn't that right? Now, you might be wondering how you do the diving in such a game, so we invite you to find out by reading this next part of the description carefully. Well, with the mouse you are going to hold the left mouse button, and then release it when you are ready to dive. From the bottom you have buttons with which you can change the diver, or the tricks that you do while in the air. When in the air, click once again on the left mouse button in order to straighten out. You get coins depending on how well you did the flips and the tricks. Try your best each time, and have spectacular fun, like you are only able to have while on our website!
