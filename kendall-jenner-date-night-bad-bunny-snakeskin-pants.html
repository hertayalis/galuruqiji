Kendall Jenner Does Date Night in Some Incredible Snakeskin Pants

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Date night for Kendall Jenner and Bad Bunny is sure to generate some sartorial inspiration for your next romantic outing. Case in point: The model and rapper’s most recent date had the couple dressed in coordinating white looks. Paparazzi captured photos of the two getting sushi with friends last night in West Hollywood.

Jenner looked cool in a fitted white T-shirt paired with high-waisted flared trousers emblazoned with a trippy black-and-white snakeskin print. She cinched her pants at her waist with a black belt, then further accessorized with a black rectangular shoulder bag and black square-toe boots.

Bad Bunny (born Benito Antonio Martínez Ocasio) also wore a white T-shirt, though he opted for more subdued bottoms by wearing a pair of off-white trousers with a light gray print. For a pop of color, the Grammy winner topped off the look with a camouflage trucker hat, which he wore backwards, as well as a few chain necklaces and a gold watch.

Jenner and Bad Bunny, who were first romantically linked in February, previously showed off their couple style while sitting courtside at an NBA playoff match in Los Angeles last month.

At the time, the 818 founder also relied on snakeskin to make a fashion statement, wearing knee-high stiletto boots covered in an amber snakeskin print. She paired the shoes with a white tank top, a brown miniskirt, and chunky gold hoop earrings.

The musician wore his own pair of snakeskin boots to the game, too—a white cowboy-boot iteration. He additionally wore a white collared button-up, a black leather jacket, black trousers, and a black baseball cap.
