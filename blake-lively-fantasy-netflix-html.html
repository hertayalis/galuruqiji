The Blake Lively Fantasy Movie On Netflix Is Her Best Work

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, May 21, 2024

By Steven Nelson | Published 5 months ago

Ever wondered about the perks and pitfalls of eternal youth? Step into the enchanting realm of The Age of Adaline, where Blake Lively graces the screen in a timeless tale of beauty and heartache. As we’ve seen her illuminate various roles with her undeniable charm, Lively takes on a character who’s quite literally ageless, navigating the complexities of life while remaining 29 forever.

Blake Lively stars in The Age of Adaline streaming on Netflix

This isn’t your average romantic drama; it’s a century-long journey compacted into a cinematic gem. Let’s journey through the decades with Adaline, discovering love, loss, and the intricate dance of time, all while enjoying Blake Lively’s captivating performance.

In The Age of Adaline, Blake Lively portrays Adaline Bowman, a woman born at the turn of the 20th century who experiences a miraculous phenomenon: after a near-fatal accident, she stops aging.

From that fateful day, Adaline remains a youthful 29-year-old, even as the world around her progresses and her loved ones grow old. With the unusual gift comes a curse, as Adaline is forced to watch the people she loves age and pass away while she remains untouched by time.

Blake Lively as Adaline remains a youthful 29-year-old, even as the world around her progresses and her loved ones grow old

To keep her secret safe, Blake Lively as Adaline maintains a solitary life, avoiding close relationships and moving frequently. She changes her identity every decade to avoid suspicion and has only allowed her daughter, Flemming (portrayed by Ellen Burstyn), to know the truth about her condition.

However, Adaline’s closely guarded life takes a dramatic turn when she meets a charismatic philanthropist, Ellis Jones (Michiel Huisman). As they fall deeply in love, Adaline grapples with the choice of revealing her secret to Ellis or once again running from intimacy.

The stakes are raised when she encounters Ellis’ father, William (Harrison Ford), who recognizes her from his past, further threatening the life she has so carefully constructed.

The Age of Adaline beautifully interweaves romance and mystery, exploring themes of time, love, and the challenges of immortality. Blake Lively delivers a heartfelt performance, embodying Adaline’s wisdom, melancholy, and enduring hope.

The Age of Adaline received mixed reviews upon its release. Critics generally praised Blake Lively’s performance, highlighting her ability to convey the depth and intricacies of a character who has lived for over a century while physically remaining in her late twenties. Blake Lively’s portrayal of Adaline’s unique combination of youthful appearance and old-world elegance, together with her wisdom gained over the years, was often commended.

The film’s premise was recognized for its originality, and the narrative’s romantic and fantasy elements appealed to many. The cinematography and period-specific details, illustrating the different eras of Adaline’s life, were also points of praise.

However, some critics felt that the story, while intriguing in concept, was occasionally bogged down by its own melodrama. Some believed that the film’s potential was somewhat constrained by its commitment to the romantic genre conventions, leading to predictability in certain plot points.

In summary, while The Age of Adaline might not have been universally lauded, Blake Lively’s captivating performance and the film’s unique premise ensured that it found a dedicated audience who appreciated its blend of romance, drama, and fantasy.

The Age of Adaline stands out not just for its enthralling narrative, but also for the grace and elegance Blake Lively brings to the screen. The film offers viewers a mesmerizing journey through time, with romance and mystery intertwining seamlessly. As Adaline grapples with the blessings and burdens of her condition, we too are invited to reflect on the nature of time, love, and the human experience. 

Blake Lively’s heartfelt performance, combined with the movie’s visual splendor and poignant storytelling, make this film a must-watch on Netflix. Whether you’re in the mood for a timeless love story or a thought-provoking fantasy, The Age of Adaline promises an enchanting cinematic experience.
