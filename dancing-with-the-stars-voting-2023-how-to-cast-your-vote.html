How to Vote for Dancing with the Stars?

Casting your vote for Dancing with the Stars is a breeze. You can either vote online or via text. For online voting, simply create a login and follow the instructions on DWTS Vote, ABC, or Disney+ before the voting window closes. Each couple can receive up to 10 votes through online means. Opting for text voting? Just send the first name of the celebrity you want to support to the number 21523.

For instance, to vote for Adrian Peterson, send “ADRIAN” to 21523. Text voting allows up to 10 votes per couple. Remember, both online and text voting are available only during the live simulcast on ABC and Disney+ in the Eastern and Central time zones. Prepare yourself to vote and rally behind your cherished dancers to keep them in the running!

DWTS Voting by Text :

  • Adrian Peterson and Britt Stewart: Text ADRIAN to 21523

  • Alyson Hannigan and Sasha Farber: Text ALYSON to 21523

Disney Plus Dancing with the Stars Voting

To support your favorite dancing couple on Disney Plus’s Dancing with the Stars, head to disneyplus.com/vote. After signing in with your Disney account, select the dancer or dancers you want to back and click “DONE.” You can vote for up to 10 couples during the voting period, commencing when the live show begins and concluding when the show ends.

Both online and SMS voting are open to viewers in the United States, Puerto Rico, and Canada. Please note that voting is not accessible during rebroadcasts or on-demand streams.

Dancing with the Stars 

Dancing with the Stars has been captivating audiences since its debut on June 1, 2005, airing on ABC. Inspired by the UK series Strictly Come Dancing, the American version brings together celebrities and professional dancers for enthralling performances. Each week, the couple with the lowest combined total of judges’ points and audience votes gets eliminated, eventually leading to the crowning of a champion dance pair. The show has featured various hosts, including Tom Bergeron, Samantha Harris, Brooke Burke-Charvet, Erin Andrews, Tyra Banks, Alfonso Ribeiro, and Julianne Hough.
