STAYC; Members, Heights, Birthdays, Careers

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, July 21, 2024

STAYC is a Korean entertainment group consisting of 6 unique members formed by Black Eyed Pilseung under High Up Entertainment. STAYC made their debut on 12th November, 2020. The group members of STAYC are as follows; Sieun, Sumin, Seeun, Isa, J, and Yoon. The name of the group means Star To A Young Culture. Their dream was to become stars and rule the young culture.
