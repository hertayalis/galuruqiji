'Love &amp; Hip Hop' couple Bambi and Lil Scrappy rumored to have split after 5 years of marriage

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, September 1, 2024

ATLANTA, GEORGIA: It looks like another celebrity couple is going splitsville. After five years of marriage, Lil Scrappy and Bambi, stars of 'Love & Hip Hop', have reportedly called it quits. Sources close to the couple have confirmed their split, leaving fans of the show wondering what might have gone wrong.

Bambi and Lil Scappy's relationship has been a subject of speculation recently, as the two have stopped following each other on Instagram and removed photos of them together. On a closer look, it can be observed that there are no recent photos on Bambi's profile that feature Scrappy. It's believed that the rumors of their split were further confirmed when it was reported that they spent New Year's Eve separately. While the couple has yet to make an official statement, it appears that the end of their romance is imminent.

In 2017, the Jasmine Brand reported that Lil Scrappy and Bambi had tied the knot in a secret ceremony. The following month, Lil Scrappy confirmed the news and revealed that the wedding was an intimate affair. Scrappy and Bambi are proud parents to three adorable children, three-year-old Breland, two-year-old Xylo, and one-year-old Cali. In addition, Scrappy has a daughter from a previous relationship, Emani Richardson, 17, whom he shares with his ex-fiance and former 'Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta' star, Erica Dixon.

Viewers of 'Love & Hip Hop Atlanta' have seen the tumultuous relationship between Scrappy and Bambi. From Momma Dee's interference to heated conversations about divorce, the couple have experienced their fair share of ups and downs. This rollercoaster of a relationship has kept fans of the show on the edge of their seat, eagerly anticipating the couple's next move. According to Pop Culture, "A source close to production alleges Scrappy may be dating his co-star, Erica Mena, who is the ex-wife of Safaree Samuels."
