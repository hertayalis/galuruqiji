Who is Paul Costabile?

Paul Costabile’s Early & Professional Career

In 2007, he started his career as a standup comedian in the theatre, The Upright Citizen’s Brigade Theatre. Following that, he started broadcasting on various talk shows in Times Square, NYC.

After two years, in 2009, he created his own Late Night Talk Show, Gorilla Late Night. Later, he started working for the radio network, iHeartRadio as a host. For the radio station, he hosted the Now Signature, off-the-cut artist interviews.

Also, he has worked as a writer, and host, and produced various videos for Madison Square Garden and The Hollywood Reporter. Following that, he hosted Animal Planet’s Risk It for the Biscuit. 

Also, he conducted interviews at various Award shows like The Grammy, The Oscars, and SAG Awards.
