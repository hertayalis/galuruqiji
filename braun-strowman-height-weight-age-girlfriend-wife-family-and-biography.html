Braun Strowman: Height, Weight, Age, Girlfriend, Wife, Family, and Biography

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, August 9, 2024

Discover everything you need to know about Braun Strowman, the renowned WWE wrestler who has captured the wrestling world’s attention with his imposing physique and dominating presence. Since his debut, he has garnered a considerable fan base owing to his previous career as a Strongman.

Biography & Profile Summary

Born on September 6, 1983, Braun Strowman, also known as The Titan, hails from Sherrills Ford, North Carolina. He is 39 years old (as of 2023) and holds American nationality. Strowman’s upbringing involved spending significant time in Arkansas and Tennessee. He is recognized for his activities during high school, where he participated in football, track, and field at Bandys High School.

Braun Strowman Height & Weight

Standing at an impressive height of 6 feet 8 inches (203 cm) and weighing around 175 kg (385 lbs), Braun Strowman exudes an imposing presence in the ring. His physique boasts a chest measurement of 62 inches, a waist of 42 inches, and biceps measuring 24 inches. Additionally, he possesses long dark brown hair and dark brown eyes.

Braun Strowman Career & Wrestling

Before his WWE career, Strowman held roles as a mechanic and a security guard. However, his journey into the wrestling world began when he transitioned from Strongman competitions to catching the eye of WWE scout Mark Henry in 2012. This led to his NXT debut, subsequent appearances, and eventual prominence as one of the most dominant wrestlers in WWE.

Braun Strowman Parents, Siblings & Family

Braun Strowman’s parents are Rick “Crusher” and Sara Scherr. His father, Rick “Crusher” Scherr, is a renowned slow-pitch softball player and a member of the USSSA Hall of Fame. His mother, Sara Scherr, is a homemaker. Strowman also has a younger sister named Hannah Scherr. Additionally, he was named the godfather of Bray Wyatt and JoJo Offerman’s son, Nash Rotunda.

Braun Strowman Wife

Braun hails from a sports-oriented family, with his father’s sporting legacy influencing his own career. While currently unmarried, he is reportedly in a relationship with wrestler Kailey Dawn Farmer, also known as Kamilla Kaine.

Braun Strowman Net Worth

As of 2023, Braun Strowman’s net worth is estimated to be $2 million, garnered through his successful professional wrestling career.
