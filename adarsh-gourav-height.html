Who is Adarsh Gourav? - Adarsh Gourav Wiki

Adarsh Gourav Wiki: - Adarsh Gourav is an Indian actor. He was born in Jamshedpur, Bihar, India, on July 8, 1994. He started his acting career at the age of 14. In 2010, Gourav made his acting debut in the drama film My Name Is Khan. In 2020, he gained notoriety for playing Balram Halwai in the satirical movie The White Tiger. He starred in Rukh and Mom in 2017.

He participated in the TVF production Hostel Daze and the Amazon production "Die Trying" after graduating from college. He collaborated with Deepa Mehta, an Academy Award contender, on her Netflix series Leila in 2018. He participated in the Desi Down Under podcast series on Audible in May 2023. In 2023, he also starred in the comedic thriller Guns & Gulaabs.
